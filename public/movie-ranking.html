The Best Movies According to Utkarsh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Need for Speed (2014)

A street racer embarks on a cross-country journey of revenge and redemption after being framed for a crime.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

An innocent man forms an enduring bond with a fellow inmate as he navigates hope and perseverance in a harsh prison.

Weathering With You (2019)

A romance about a boy and a girl with the power to control the weather.
